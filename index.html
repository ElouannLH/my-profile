Ski

Living close to the mountains and resorts in Haute Savoie, I've always done a lot of skiing every winter. I did it in a club when I was younger, and now I continue to go as much as I can every winter, especially with my friends. It's such a different feeling from most other sports, in the right conditions, I think it's my favourite thing to do.
